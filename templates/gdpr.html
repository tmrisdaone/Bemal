Our commitment to data protection

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Data Processing
Neon Outreach acts as both a Data Controller and Data Processor under GDPR. We process personal data in accordance with GDPR requirements.
Lawful Basis
We process data based on: (1) contractual necessity, (2) legitimate interest, (3) consent where required, and (4) legal compliance.
Data Subject Rights
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
Data Protection Officer
Contact our DPO at dpo@neonoutreach.com for any GDPR-related inquiries.

For Our Customers

When using Neon Outreach to process personal data of EU residents, you are considered the Data Controller under GDPR. We provide tools to help you comply:

  • Data processing agreements (DPA) available upon request
  • Options to pseudonymize prospect data
  • Automated deletion tools for expired data
  • Audit logs of all data processing activities
